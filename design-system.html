SHAPE PRINCIPLE No pill-shaped buttons (999px radius) — those read as tech/corporate. Buttons are rectangular with warmth (8px). The Mughal arch (pointed at top) is a recurring motif drawn from the moodboard — India Gate, Heleena's frames, the existing door — and should be used for special hero elements, not generic UI.
